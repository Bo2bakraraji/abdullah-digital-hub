Common questions

Before we start.

Do I need a large advertising budget?+

No. The first goal is to make sure the offer and customer journey deserve a larger budget. We can begin with a focused pilot and define the budget before anything launches.

Can you guarantee sales?+

No honest marketer can guarantee sales. I can guarantee a clear setup, transparent work, tracked actions, and decisions based on what the test teaches us.

What happens during the free growth check?+

We look at your business, current online presence, offer, and main goal. You leave with the most important next step—even if we decide not to work together.

Do you work only with businesses in Lebanon?+

Lebanese local businesses are the current focus because local context matters, but online projects can also be considered when the fit is right.
